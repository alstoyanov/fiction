# The Divided Truth - Concise Plot

## Story Type
Short Story #3 - Introduces the Volkov Triplets

## Core Concept
Three identical triplets—an enforcer, an outlaw, and a technician—are torn apart by the Histologic system. Living near the Veridica-Chronos border, they experience fact wars firsthand. When their mother dies during a fact storm, all three end up in correction facility despite being on different sides.

## Main Characters
- **Dmitri Volkov** (32): Enforcer, rigid believer, hunting his brother
- **Alexei Volkov** (32): Outlaw operative, "The Phantom"
- **Nikolai Volkov** (32): Factorepo technician, secret helper to both
- **Elena Volkov** (Mother): Dying, wants sons reunited
- **Captain Sokolov**: Border security investigating fact disputes

## Plot Summary

**Part 1: Three Separate Men**
- Dmitri hunts "The Phantom" (not knowing it's his brother)
- Alexei helps recruits escape the system
- Nikolai maintains factorepo systems, hiding evidence
- Story doesn't reveal they're triplets initially
- All three called to mother's deathbed

**Part 2: The Truth Revealed**
- Hospital room: three identical faces
- Flashbacks show their childhood and father's death in fact storm
- Mother dies during another fact storm
- Alexei reveals evidence of fact storm weapons
- Dmitri torn between duty and family

**Part 3: The Capture**
- Enforcers raid hospital
- Brothers run together for first time in years
- Surrounded in border zone during fact storm
- All three captured
- Sent to correction facility

**Epilogue: One Year Later**
- Three brothers in separate but visible cells
- Correction failing on them—triplet bond too strong
- Nikolai secretly preserving their memories
- They remain connected despite separation

## Key Themes
- Family vs. ideology
- Border zones and fact wars
- Fact storms as weapons
- Triplet bond that can't be broken
- Three perspectives on same truth

## Unique Elements
- Fact storms: reality flickering between contradictory versions
- Border zone: contested space where facts overlap
- Triplet reveal in Part 2
- All three protagonists, no clear hero/villain

## Ending
All three in correction facility but uncorrectable. Their bond proves stronger than the system. Nikolai secretly works to preserve what they are.

## Connection to Series
- Introduces all three Volkov brothers
- Establishes fact wars and border conflicts
- Shows correction facility from inside
- Nikolai's hidden data crucial in later books
- Elena (aunt) becomes major character in novels

## Timeline
~1 year before "The Correction"
