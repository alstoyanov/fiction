# The Stolen Fact - Concise Plot

## Story Type
Short Story #2 - Introduces Kira Osman

## Core Concept
Meticulous factorepo technician Kira Osman reports a stolen fact—one she's certain she recorded has vanished. She becomes the prime suspect in her own case, manipulated by an outlaw recruiter who uses seduction to plant doubt. Despite being a careful believer, she's convicted and sent to correction facility.

## Main Characters
- **Kira Osman** (27): Factorepo technician, meticulous, precise
- **Adrian Kovač**: Outlaw recruiter posing as fellow technician
- **Supervisor Chen**: Kira's boss, initially supportive then suspicious
- **The Judge**: AI system that convicts her

## Plot Summary

**Act 1: The Discovery**
- Kira discovers a fact she recorded is missing from factorepo
- She reports it through proper channels
- Investigation begins but finds no evidence of the fact ever existing
- Kira becomes increasingly paranoid

**Act 2: The Manipulation**
- Adrian Kovač befriends her, seems sympathetic
- He subtly plants doubts about the system
- Kira resists but begins questioning
- Adrian is actually outlaw recruiter testing her
- He manipulates evidence to make her look guilty

**Act 3: The Conviction**
- Kira accused of fabricating the stolen fact
- The Judge rules she's been contaminated by outlaw ideology
- Adrian disappears, mission accomplished
- Kira sent to correction facility
- She still believes in Histologic but questions Veridica's implementation

## Key Themes
- Precision vs. doubt
- System vulnerability
- Manipulation through sympathy
- The stolen fact as metaphor for stolen certainty

## Ending
Kira arrives at correction facility, confused and betrayed but not broken. She knows something is wrong with the system but doesn't know what.

## Connection to Series
- Introduces Kira who becomes Marcus's partner
- Establishes "stolen fact" concept used in later novels
- Shows system can be manipulated from inside
- Kira's technical expertise crucial in all later books
