# Geographic Correction - SFW and CNT Territories

## Issue Identified

The original geographic distribution placed:
- **SFW**: South America
- **CNT**: Russia and Central Asia

**Problem**: These nations were described as "bitter enemies" with active monthly border skirmishes, but they had no shared border, making military conflict geographically impossible.

## Solution Implemented

### New Geographic Distribution

**SFW (Sovereignty of Free Will)**
- **Territory**: Middle East, North Africa, and South Asia
- **Specific regions**: Iran, Turkey, Egypt, Arabian Peninsula, Pakistan, Afghanistan
- **Population**: 250 million
- **Strategic resources**: Oil, natural gas

**CNT (Confederacy of Natural Truth)**
- **Territory**: Russia, Central Asia, Caucasus, and parts of Eastern Europe
- **Specific regions**: Russia, Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, Turkmenistan, Georgia, Armenia, Azerbaijan
- **Population**: 300 million
- **Strategic resources**: Oil, natural gas, minerals

### Shared Border

**The War Zone**: Massive shared border stretching thousands of kilometers from:
- **Western end**: Black Sea and Caucasus region (Georgia, Armenia, Azerbaijan)
- **Through**: Central Asia (Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, Turkmenistan)
- **Eastern end**: Afghanistan/Turkmenistan border region

**Primary Conflict Zone**: Former Afghanistan/Turkmenistan border region - most intense fighting

## Why This Works Better

### 1. Geographic Logic
- ✅ Shared border enables realistic military conflict
- ✅ Border stretches thousands of kilometers (multiple conflict zones possible)
- ✅ Mountainous terrain (Caucasus, Hindu Kush) creates natural defensive positions
- ✅ Resource-rich territories (oil, gas, water) provide realistic conflict motivations

### 2. Historical Resonance
- ✅ Caucasus region has historical religious/ethnic tensions
- ✅ Central Asia has history as contested territory
- ✅ Afghanistan region has long history of conflict
- ✅ Oil and gas resources have driven real-world conflicts in this region

### 3. Cultural Logic
- ✅ SFW's religious foundation (Islamic, Christian, other faiths) fits Middle East/North Africa
- ✅ CNT's authoritarian structure fits former Soviet territories
- ✅ Religious vs. atheist conflict has historical precedent in this region
- ✅ Democratic vs. authoritarian clash mirrors real historical tensions

### 4. Strategic Resources
- ✅ Both nations control major oil and gas reserves
- ✅ Water rights (Caspian Sea, rivers) create additional conflict
- ✅ Control of energy exports to Histologic nations creates economic competition
- ✅ Strategic position between Europe and Asia

### 5. Story Potential Enhanced
- ✅ Border war stories now geographically realistic
- ✅ Smuggling routes through mountain passes
- ✅ Divided families across border
- ✅ Resource wars over oil, gas, water
- ✅ Religious vs. atheist conflict
- ✅ Romeo & Juliet stories across war zone
- ✅ Refugee journeys through dangerous terrain
