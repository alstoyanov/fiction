## Steampunk Aesthetic

### Core Visual Elements

**Materials**:
- Brass (primary metal - gold tones)
- Copper (secondary - orange-red tones)
- Bronze (accents)
- Leather (books, clothing)
- Crystal/Glass (displays, windows)

**Technology**:
- Visible gears and clockwork mechanisms
- Steam vents and pipes
- Pneumatic tubes for data transport
- Brass cylinders for fact storage
- Amber tubes for displays (instead of screens)
- Gaslight for illumination

**Architecture**:
- Victorian-Gothic buildings
- Brass and copper construction
- Exposed machinery
- Steam towers
- Ornate metalwork and filigree

**Fashion**:
- Victorian-era clothing
- Brass buttons and accessories
- Copper jewelry and trim
- Brass goggles (status symbol)
- Leather vests and coats

### Lighting

**Primary**: Gaslight (warm amber glow)
**Secondary**: Moonlight (cool silver)
**Accents**: Amber displays, brass reflections
**Atmosphere**: Fog and steam creating depth

### Color Palette

**Warm Metals**: 
- Brass: #B8860B (dark goldenrod)
- Copper: #B87333 (copper)
- Bronze: #CD7F32 (bronze)

**Accent Colors**:
- Deep burgundy: #800020
- Emerald green: #50C878
- Midnight blue: #191970
- Amber glow: #FFBF00

**Atmospheric**:
- Fog gray: #D3D3D3
- Steam white: #F5F5F5
- Shadow black: #1C1C1C

## Using the Prompts

### For Midjourney
1. Copy entire prompt including technical parameters
2. Use `--ar 16:9` for landscape, `--ar 2:3` for portrait
3. Use `--style raw` for more detailed mechanical elements
4. Add `--chaos 20-30` for more creative interpretations
5. Current version: `--v 6` (adjust as needed)

### For DALL-E / Stable Diffusion
1. Remove Midjourney-specific parameters (`--ar`, `--style`, `--v`)
2. Keep descriptive text
3. Add "steampunk, Victorian era, brass and copper, highly detailed, 8k"
4. May need to emphasize "visible gears" and "mechanical details"

### For Other AI Generators
1. Focus on descriptive elements
2. Emphasize "steampunk aesthetic"
3. Specify "brass, copper, Victorian era"
4. Add "gaslight, steam, fog" for atmosphere

## Prompt Structure

Each prompt follows this structure:

```
[Style description] scene of [subject] [doing action]. 
[Key visual elements with materials]. 
[Lighting description]. 
[Atmospheric elements]. 
[Mood and composition notes]. 
[Technical parameters for Midjourney]
```

### Example:
```
Steampunk fantasy scene of a young Asian man in Victorian-era 
clothing with brass goggles, operating an enormous brass and 
copper factorepo machine. Massive mechanical apparatus with 
spinning gears, rotating data cylinders, and steam vents. 
Warm gaslight illumination. Hundreds of small brass tubes 
pneumatically transporting fact-capsules. Rich detail on 
brass fixtures and copper piping. 16:9 cinematic composition. 
--ar 16:9 --style raw --v 6
```

## Creating Your Own Prompts

### Template:
```
Steampunk [scene type] of [character description] 
[action/pose]. [Setting details with brass/copper]. 
[Mechanical elements - gears, steam, etc.]. 
[Lighting - gaslight, amber, etc.]. 
[Atmospheric elements - fog, steam]. 
[Mood and composition]. 
[Aspect ratio]. --ar [ratio] --style raw --v 6
```

### Key Words to Include:
- "Steampunk" or "Victorian era"
- "Brass" and "copper" (materials)
- "Gears" or "clockwork" (mechanisms)
- "Gaslight" (lighting)
- "Steam" or "fog" (atmosphere)
- "Gothic-industrial" (architecture)

### What Makes a Good Steampunk Prompt:
1. **Visible Mechanisms**: Always show gears, pipes, mechanisms
2. **Material Specificity**: Brass, copper, bronze, leather
3. **Lighting**: Gaslight creates warm, dramatic atmosphere
4. **Atmosphere**: Fog and steam add depth and mood
5. **Victorian Fashion**: Period-appropriate clothing with brass accessories
6. **Ornate Details**: Filigree, engravings, decorative metalwork
