## 1. THOMAS WEI - The Murderer

### Novel Appearances
- **Novel 05**: "The Lost Hour" - Main antagonist, orchestrates erasure

### Basic Information
- **Age**: 30
- **Role**: Government insider, orchestrates Lost Hour
- **Family**: Son of Dr. Wei Zhang (Architect)
- **Alignment**: Neutral Evil (sociopathic)

### Personality & Traits
- Brilliant, sociopathic, proud
- No empathy
- Sees people as variables
- Arrogant about his crime

### Background
Learned from father (Architect) that people are controllable. Took it further. Murdered Minister Kozlov who was about to expose corruption. Erased the hour to hide evidence.

### Character Arc
**Novel 05**: Antagonist
- Murders Minister Viktor Kozlov (14:17 during lost hour)
- Orchestrates factorepo erasure (14:00-15:00)
- Frames it as system failure
- Caught when investigation reveals deliberate erasure
- Proud of his crime: "I erased an hour of history. I'm a god."

### Key Quotes
- "I erased an hour of history. I'm a god."
- "My father taught me people are variables. I solved for X."
- "The Judge can't judge what it doesn't know happened."

### Fate
Convicted, sent to correction facility, remains unrepentant

### Alignment
Neutral Evil (pure sociopath)
