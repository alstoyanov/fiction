## SPECIAL CHARACTER TYPES

### The Wise Speakers (Provide Meaningful Truths)

**Dr. Yuki Tanaka** - The Healer
- **Background**: Decades of medical practice, seen human suffering
- **Teaching Style**: Medical metaphors, healing wisdom
- **Sample Quotes**:
  - "A wound must be acknowledged before it can heal"
  - "The body remembers what the mind forgets"
  - "Pain is information. Listen to it."
  - "Scars are proof of survival, not failure"

**Isaiah Okonkwo** - The Chronicler
- **Background**: Journalist, historian, observer
- **Teaching Style**: Historical parallels, documented truth
- **Sample Quotes**:
  - "History doesn't repeat, but it rhymes"
  - "The first casualty of war is truth"
  - "Those who control the past control the future"
  - "I don't make history. I record it. The difference matters."

**The Counselor** (AI) - The Advisor
- **Background**: Successor to The Judge, learned from its sacrifice
- **Teaching Style**: Computational wisdom, probabilistic insights
- **Sample Quotes**:
  - "I calculate possibilities. You choose probabilities."
  - "The Judge knew everything. I know enough."
  - "Certainty is the enemy of wisdom"
  - "I advise. You decide. That's not weakness. That's design."

**Professor Adrian Volkov** (Architect) - The Academic
- **Background**: Historian, scholar, regretful conspirator
- **Teaching Style**: Academic knowledge, historical context
- **Sample Quotes**:
  - "We created a system to prevent war. We created a system that required it."
  - "The road to tyranny is paved with good intentions"
  - "I taught my nephews history. I should have taught them to question it."

### The Humorous Characters

**Alexei Volkov** - Gallows Humor
- Makes jokes in dangerous situations
- "If we die, at least the funeral will be well-documented"
- "I've escaped this facility before. Second time's easier. Or third. I've lost count."
- Light in dark moments

**Kira Osman** - Technical Sarcasm
- Dry observations about systems and people
- "The system isn't broken. It's working as designed—badly."
- "I can hack a factorepo but not human stupidity"
- Humor emerges under stress

**Isaiah Okonkwo** - Observational Wit
- Sees absurdity in situations
- "We're fighting a war over which version of history is correct. The irony is historical."
- "I'm documenting the end of certainty. Ironically, I'm certain about that."
