## Ideology & Beliefs

"First, do no harm. Then, do some good."

### Key Beliefs
- Every person deserves dignity
- Healing requires acknowledging wounds
- The body and mind are connected
- Ethics cannot be compromised
- Suffering teaches, but shouldn't define

### Meaningful Teachings (The Wise Speaker)

**On Healing**:
- "A wound must be acknowledged before it can heal"
- "The body remembers what the mind forgets"
- "Scars are proof of survival, not failure"
- "Healing isn't linear. Neither is life."
- "Pain is information. Listen to it."

**On Ethics**:
- "The moment you compromise your ethics, you lose yourself"
- "Good intentions don't excuse harmful actions"
- "We are defined by what we refuse to do"

**On Humanity**:
- "We're all broken. That's what makes us human."
- "Compassion costs nothing and heals everything"
- "The strongest people are those who help others despite their own pain"

- "A system that harms people to help them isn't helping"

**On Truth**:

- "The truth can hurt, but lies hurt more"

## Character Arc

### Novel 04: "The Correction"
**Role**: Major character, moral conscience
**Arc**: Professional → Whistleblower → Helper
- Refuses to perform correction on innocent patient
- Convicted for ethical stance
- Observes other inmates, assesses psychological states
- Realizes they're all "uncorrectable"—they've seen truth
- Helps plan escape
- **Key Moment**: "I took an oath: First, do no harm. I'm keeping it."

### Novel 05: "The Lost Hour"
**Role**: Supporting character
**Arc**: Helper → Accused → Defender
- Helps family of defectors escape to Chronos during lost hour
- Accused of human trafficking
- Defends defectors' choice to flee
- Cleared when defectors testify
- Becomes advocate for ethical defection
- **Key Moment**: "They came willingly. They wanted freedom. That's not trafficking. That's helping."

### Novel 07: "Battle of Blood"
**Role**: Major character, witness
**Arc**: Healer → Witness → Chronicler
- Field surgeon near front lines
- Treats wounded: soldiers and civilians
- Documents human cost of war
- Provides medical and emotional support
- Writes memoir during war
- **Key Moment**: Treating dying soldier: "You're not alone. I'm here."

### Novel 08: "Architects of Chaos"
**Role**: Supporting character
**Arc**: Witness → Betrayed → Advocate
- Discovers sister's superior (Maya Tanaka) is an Architect
- Confronts Maya: "You're my sister. How could you?"
- Helps expose Architects
- Continues healing work
- **Key Moment**: "I heal people. You controlled them. We're not the same."
