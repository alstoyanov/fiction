## Travel Routes

### The Diplomatic Circuit
```

```
- Most common diplomatic route
- Uses Synthesis as hub (only nation maintaining relations with all)
- Heavily monitored at each stop
- Requires extensive factorepo synchronization

### The Refugee Trail (Histologic ‚Üí SFW)
```
VERIDICA/CHRONOS ‚Üí Neutral Zones ‚Üí SFW Border ‚Üí Refugee Camps
```
- Dangerous journey through militarized zones
- Many die attempting crossing
- SFW grants asylum for propaganda purposes
- Difficult integration (Histologic mindset vs. free will culture)

### The Outlaw Underground
```
Any Histologic Nation ‚Üí Smuggling Networks ‚Üí Synthesis OR SFW
```

- Illegal routes through black markets
- High cost, high risk

## Story Setting Guide

### Want Isolated, Claustrophobic Setting?
- **Veridica**: Rigid surveillance, no escape
- **Chronos**: Neural interface prevents privacy
- **Axiom**: Logic traps, no emotional outlet

### Want Cultural Richness?
- **Memoria**: Art, philosophy, human connection
- **SFW**: Religious communities, oral traditions

### Want Political Intrigue?
- **Synthesis**: Diplomatic hub, spies everywhere
- **Special Economic Zones**: Black markets, smuggling

### Want War/Action?
- **SFW-CNT Border**: Active combat
- **Great Lakes**: Fact war escalation
- **Any border**: Smuggling, refugees, guards

### Want Philosophical Exploration?
- **Axiom**: Pure logic vs. human nature
- **Memoria**: Art and truth
- **Synthesis**: Multiple truths coexisting

### Want Fish Out of Water?
- Veridica citizen in SFW (surveillance to freedom)
- SFW citizen in Chronos (privacy to total exposure)
- CNT citizen in Axiom (chaos to pure logic)

### Want Romance Across Divides?
- Any cross-border romance is forbidden and dangerous
- Greatest tension: Histologic ‚Üê‚Üí Non-Histologic
- Most tragic: SFW ‚Üê‚Üí CNT (literally impossible)

## Geographic Consistency Checklist

When writing stories, ensure:
- [ ] Characters' origin nation affects their worldview
- [ ] Travel between nations is appropriately difficult
- [ ] Border crossings are significant events
- [ ] Geographic distance matters (can't easily travel between continents)
- [ ] Climate and resources affect plot
- [ ] Population distribution is realistic
- [ ] Military capabilities match nation's resources
- [ ] Technology level matches nation's philosophy
- [ ] Cultural differences reflect geographic separation
- [ ] Historical events shaped current borders
