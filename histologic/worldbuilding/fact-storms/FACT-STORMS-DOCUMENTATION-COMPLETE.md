## ðŸ”¬ Technical Details Established

### How Fact Storms Work

**Natural Occurrence** (Rare):
1. Two factorepos contain contradictory facts about same location
2. Both systems attempt to enforce their version of reality
3. Physical space cannot reconcile incompatible histories
4. Reality becomes unstable â†’ fact storm

**Manufactured Occurrence** (Weaponized):
1. Operatives deliberately introduce contradictory facts
2. Facts designed for maximum cognitive dissonance
3. Timing, location, intensity precisely controlled
4. Collateral damage calculated and accepted

### Elena Volkov's Control Techniques

- **Radius Control**: 2km (small) to 5km+ (large)
- **Duration Control**: 10-20 minutes typical
- **Intensity Calibration**: Adjustable severity
- **Timing Precision**: Exact moment triggering
- **Collateral Minimization**: Learning to reduce civilian casualties
- **Limitation**: Always causes some collateral damage

### Medical Understanding

**Neural Damage Patterns**:
- Hippocampus (memory formation) most affected
- Prefrontal cortex (decision-making) severely impacted
- Temporal lobe (reality perception) shows degradation
- Amygdala (fear response) becomes hyperactive
- White matter lesions similar to dementia

**Prognosis**:
- Mild exposure: Full recovery possible
- Moderate exposure: Partial recovery with treatment
- Severe exposure: Permanent damage likely
- Chronic exposure: Progressive decline, no cure
