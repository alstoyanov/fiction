# Fact Storms: Quick Reference

## Definition

**Fact storms** are catastrophic events where contradictory historical facts from competing factorepos overlap in physical space, causing reality instability and severe cognitive damage. Initially believed natural, later revealed as **weaponized psychological warfare**.

---

## Physical Effects

### Visual
- Buildings flicker between different states (brass/stone/glass, exists/demolished)
- Sky changes colors (blue/red/green/black)
- Time feels distorted (too fast/slow/"sideways")
- Steam vents, gaslights, fixtures phase in/out of existence
- Spatial distortion (distances wrong, paths maze-like)

### Auditory
- Reality "hums" or "buzzes"
- Mechanical sounds that shouldn't exist
- Pressure-change sensation
- Warning sirens

---

## Psychological Effects

### During Storm (by exposure time)

**< 5 minutes (Mild)**:
- Severe headaches, disorientation, nausea
- Temporary memory confusion, anxiety

**5-15 minutes (Moderate)**:
- Intense migraine-level pain
- Competing memories of same event
- Cognitive dissonance causing physical pain
- Screaming, laughing, or catatonic states

**15+ minutes (Severe)**:
- Permanent cognitive damage
- Memory fragmentation, psychotic breaks
- Permanent catatonia, brain death (extreme cases)

### Long-Term (Chronic Exposure)
- Progressive cognitive decline
- Difficulty distinguishing truth from falsehood
- Memory degradation, personality changes
- Depression, anxiety disorders
- Eventual brain death

## How They Work

### The Transmission System

**Neural Interfaces** (mandatory for all citizens):
- Microscopic bio-integrated mesh in hippocampus and prefrontal cortex
- Continuously receives factorepo broadcasts via quantum-entangled towers
- Integrates broadcast facts directly into memory formation
- Cannot be turned off; removal causes death
- Makes factorepo information feel as "real" as sensory input

**Normal Operation**:
1. Factorepo towers broadcast location-relevant facts
2. Neural interface receives quantum-encoded stream
3. Brain integrates facts with sensory input and personal memory
4. Result: Seamless "knowledge" of historical truth

### Natural Occurrence (Rare)

**When two factorepos broadcast contradictory facts**:
1. Border zones where broadcast ranges overlap
2. Neural interface receives BOTH contradictory fact streams simultaneously
3. Brain attempts to integrate incompatible information
4. Hippocampus cannot form coherent memory from conflicting inputs
5. Neural stress causes reality perception breakdown
6. Result: Fact storm (perceptual hallucination, but feels completely real)

**Why It Causes Damage**:
- Brain literally tries to hold two contradictory memories simultaneously
- Memory formation center receives conflicting encoding signals
- Neurotransmitter cascade causes physical neural stress
- Prolonged exposure causes permanent white matter damage
- Eventually: Neural death

**Why Buildings "Flicker"**:
- Physical reality doesn't actually change
- Neural interface feeds contradictory facts about what you're seeing
- Brain rapidly switches between interpretations
- Everyone sees similar effects (same contradictory broadcasts)
- Shared hallucination makes it feel objective

### Manufactured (Weaponized)

**Operatives create storms by**:
1. Injecting contradictory facts into both factorepos
2. Synchronizing broadcasts to occur simultaneously
3. Targeting specific locations where victims will be
4. Controlling intensity via number/severity of contradictions
5. Accepting collateral damage to anyone in broadcast range

## Protection Methods

### Psychological Anchoring

These work by providing neural pathways that bypass the compromised interface:

- **Music/Song**: Sing familiar songs (especially pre-Histologic)
  - "You'll Never Walk Alone" used during escapes
  - Activates different neural pathways (auditory, motor) separate from interface
  - Occupies cognitive resources, reducing contradiction processing
  - Pre-Histologic songs have no factorepo conflicts
  
- **Physical Touch**: Hold hands, maintain contact with others
  - Tactile input bypasses neural interface entirely
  - Brain prioritizes immediate physical sensation over factorepo
  - Oxytocin release reduces panic
  
- **Memory Focus**: Concentrate on personal, unrecorded memories
  - Personal memories in organic neural pathways, not interface-mediated
  - Emotional memories especially stable (separate brain regions)
  - Strengthens sense of self independent of factorepo
  
- **Training**: Know what to expect, practice breathing
  - Pre-established neural patterns for handling contradiction
  - Prepared brain has alternative response patterns
  - Less damage because brain doesn't "fight" contradiction as hard

### Physical Protection
- **Rapid Evacuation**: Get out of affected zone quickly
- **Shelter**: Interior rooms, minimal external references
- **Sensory Limitation**: Close eyes, cover ears
- **Medical Treatment**: Post-exposure cognitive therapy

## Medical Understanding

### Neural Damage
- Hippocampus (memory) most affected
- Prefrontal cortex (decision-making) impacted
- Temporal lobe (reality perception) degraded
- White matter lesions similar to dementia

### Prognosis
- **Mild exposure**: Full recovery possible
- **Moderate**: Partial recovery with treatment
- **Severe**: Permanent damage likely
- **Chronic**: Progressive decline, no cure
