## Usage Notes

### Steampunk Mystery Elements:
- **Victorian Noir**: Detective atmosphere with gaslight and fog
- **Mechanical Archives**: Brass cylinders, clockwork retrieval, pneumatic tubes
- **Surveillance**: Clockwork orbs with rotating lenses and visible gears
- **Gothic-Industrial**: Victorian architecture with exposed machinery
- **Atmospheric Steam**: Ever-present fog and steam for mood

### Color Palette:
- **Primary**: Brass (gold), copper (orange-red), bronze
- **Accents**: Deep green (Kira's colors), midnight blue, amber glow
- **Lighting**: Gaslight (warm amber), moonlight (cool silver), fog (atmospheric white)
- **Institutional**: White-painted brass (correction facility)

### Character Consistency:
- **Kira**: Victorian work attire, brass-rimmed spectacles, practical hair with copper pins, brass rings
- **Adrian**: Gentleman's attire, brass vest buttons, copper cufflinks, brass pocket watch
- **Marcus**: Gray uniform, brass-rimmed spectacles (when they meet)

### Mood Progression:
- **Act 1**: Meticulous order, warm gaslight, beautiful machinery
- **Act 2**: Growing paranoia, fog, shadows, noir atmosphere
- **Act 3**: Harsh light, dramatic shadows, mechanical violence
- **Act 4**: Institutional coldness, white brass, oppressive cleanliness
