## Usage Notes

### Steampunk Gothic Elements:
- **Fog and Steam**: Ever-present atmospheric elements
- **Brass and Copper**: All technology and architecture
- **Victorian Fashion**: Period-appropriate with brass accessories
- **Clockwork Surveillance**: Rotating orbs with visible gears
- **Gothic Architecture**: Victorian-industrial hybrid
- **Gaslight**: Warm amber lighting throughout

### The Triplets' Visual Identity:
- **Same Face**: Identical features, same height and build
- **Different Postures**: 
  - Dmitri: Rigid, military, controlled
  - Alexei: Relaxed, fluid, open
  - Nikolai: Anxious, between the others
- **Different Clothing**:
  - Dmitri: Brass-armored enforcer uniform
  - Alexei: Civilian gentleman's attire
  - Nikolai: Technician's work clothes
  - All three: Gray uniforms in facility

### Color Palette by Section:
- **Part 1**: Fog gray, brass, amber gaslight, copper accents
- **Part 2**: Warmer tones, hospital whites, dramatic shadows
- **Part 3**: Action - steam white, brass gleaming, dramatic lighting
- **Part 4**: Institutional white-brass, cold gaslight, amber displays
- **Epilogue**: Muted colors, soft gaslight, melancholy mood

### Mood Progression:
- **Part 1**: Mystery, noir, separate worlds
- **Part 2**: Recognition, revelation, family tragedy
- **Part 3**: Action, desperation, unity in flight
- **Part 4**: Horror, isolation, endurance
- **Epilogue**: Melancholy hope, hidden resistance

### Key Symbols:
- **Fog**: Uncertainty, hidden truth, border zone
- **Steam**: System pressure, industrial oppression
- **Brass**: The mechanical world that divides them
- **Gaslight**: Warm humanity in cold machinery
- **Gears**: The system grinding them down
- **Three**: Unity despite separation
