## Usage Notes

### Steampunk Elements to Emphasize:
- **Brass and Copper**: Primary materials for all technology
- **Visible Mechanisms**: Gears, clockwork, steam pipes always visible
- **Victorian Fashion**: Period-appropriate clothing with steampunk accessories
- **Gaslight**: Warm amber lighting from ornate fixtures
- **Steam**: Atmospheric element throughout
- **Ornate Details**: Filigree, engravings, decorative metalwork

### Color Palette:
- **Warm Metals**: Brass (gold), copper (orange-red), bronze
- **Accent Colors**: Deep burgundy, emerald green, royal blue
- **Light Sources**: Amber gaslight, glowing tubes, candlelight
- **Steam**: White to amber-tinted atmospheric haze

### Consistency Tips:
- Keep Victorian fashion consistent for each character
- Brass goggles are status symbols (Marcus wears them)
- All technology should have visible mechanical components
- Steam and gaslight in every scene for atmosphere
- Clockwork surveillance orbs replace modern cameras
- Factorepo is mechanical: gears, cylinders, pneumatic tubes

### For Midjourney:
- Add "steampunk, Victorian era, brass and copper, gaslight" to reinforce style
- Use "--style raw" for more detailed mechanical elements
- Consider "--chaos 20" for more creative steampunk interpretations
