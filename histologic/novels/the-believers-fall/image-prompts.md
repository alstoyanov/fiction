## Usage Notes

### For Midjourney:
- Copy the entire prompt including the technical parameters
- Adjust `--v 6` to current Midjourney version if needed
- Use `--style raw` for more photorealistic results
- Adjust aspect ratios as needed for your layout

### For Other AI Image Generators (DALL-E, Stable Diffusion, etc.):
- Remove Midjourney-specific parameters (--ar, --style, --v)
- Keep the descriptive text
- Adjust prompts based on the specific model's strengths
- May need to add "photorealistic, 8k, highly detailed" for some models

### Color Grading Suggestions:
- **Act One (Images 1-7)**: Warm tones, golden hour, hopeful
- **Act Two (Images 8-15)**: Transition to cooler tones, dramatic shadows
- **Act Three (Images 16-20)**: Stark whites, cold blues, clinical

### Consistency Tips:
- Use the same character descriptions across all images
- Maintain consistent clothing in sequential scenes
- Keep the futuristic aesthetic grounded and believable
- Balance beauty with underlying surveillance/control themes
