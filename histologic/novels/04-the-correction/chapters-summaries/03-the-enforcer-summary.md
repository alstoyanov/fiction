# Chapter 3 Summary: The Enforcer

## POV: Dmitri Volkov

## Key Events
- Dmitri reflects on three years hunting Alexei, now both imprisoned
- Brothers reconcile over breakfast, Alexei helps Dmitri process
- Nikolai warns: Dr. Reeves calling individual sessions starting with Dmitri
- Dmitri's session with Reeves—ambiguous conversation
- Reeves hints at conspiracy, asks if Dmitri noticed Group Seven's unusual composition
- Reeves suggests Dmitri was convicted for being "too effective, too knowledgeable"
- Session ends cryptically—Reeves might be ally or setting trap
- Dmitri realizes he chose family over duty in Chronos (moment he hesitated)
- Accepts that love might matter more than law

## Character Development
- **Dmitri**: Rigid enforcer beginning to question, reconciles with brothers
- **Alexei**: Shows brotherly concern, offers wisdom
- **Nikolai**: Mediator, warns of danger
- **Dr. Reeves**: Introduced as ambiguous figure—helper or trap?

## Important Revelations
- Dmitri hesitated in Chronos when he had clear shot at Alexei
- That hesitation (choosing family) led to his conviction
- Dr. Reeves may be aware of conspiracy and trying to help
- Or Reeves may be testing them to expose resistance

## Themes Explored
- Duty vs. family
- Order vs. love
- Rules vs. conscience
- The moment of choice that defines us
