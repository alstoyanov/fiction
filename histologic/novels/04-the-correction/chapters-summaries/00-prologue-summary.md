# Prologue Summary: The Conviction

## Key Events

**Marcus's Conviction (August 1, 2106)**
- Marcus Chen convicted of ideological contamination through contact with outlaw Elena Vasquez
- Despite reporting her, The Judge determines he's been contaminated (73% deviation from baseline)
- Sentenced to Veridica Correction Facility for up to one year
- Marcus feels he did everything right but still failed

**Arrival at Facility (August 3, 2106)**
- Facility looks like peaceful campus but is actually prison for the mind
- Supervisor Kade assigns Marcus to "Group Seven" - special group of 8 inmates (usually 7)
- Marcus placed in Cell 7-H
- Kade hints that correction "has to" work

**First Group Session (August 4, 2106)**
- Eight inmates meet Director Helena Kovač
- Introductions reveal eight very different stories:
  1. **Marcus Chen**: Analyst, reported outlaw lover, convicted anyway
  2. **Kira Osman**: Technician, reported stolen fact, became suspect
  3. **Dmitri Volkov**: Enforcer, hunted outlaw brother, accused of helping him
  4. **Alexei Volkov**: Outlaw, helped people escape, proud of it
  5. **Nikolai Volkov**: Technician, helped both brothers
  6. **Dr. Yuki Tanaka**: Doctor, refused unethical correction
  7. **Isaiah Okonkwo**: Journalist, investigated fact storms
  8. **Mikhail "Old Songs" Petrov**: Historian, taught pre-Histologic wisdom through songs

## Character Introductions

**Marcus Chen**: True believer, betrayed love for system, still convicted
**Kira Osman**: Sharp, observant, wrongly accused
**Dmitri, Alexei, Nikolai Volkov**: Identical triplets with different paths
**Dr. Yuki Tanaka**: Gentle, ethical, refused to harm
**Isaiah Okonkwo**: Intelligent journalist, documented truth
**Mikhail "Old Songs"**: Elderly historian, speaks in songs and riddles
**Director Kovač**: Facility director, shows flickers of doubt

## Key Themes Introduced
- None of them look broken or contaminated
- They look like people who saw something they weren't supposed to see
- Mikhail's riddle: "What cage has no bars? What prison has no walls?"
- Silent agreement between Marcus and Kira: they'll figure this out together

## Atmosphere
- Facility designed to look peaceful but feels like prison
- Correction presented as healing but feels like control
- Group of eight very different people, all wrongly convicted
- Something is deeply wrong with the system
