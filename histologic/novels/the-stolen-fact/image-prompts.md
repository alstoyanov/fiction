## Usage Notes

### For Midjourney:
- Copy the entire prompt including the technical parameters
- Adjust `--v 6` to current Midjourney version if needed
- Use `--style raw` for more photorealistic results
- Architectural shots benefit from `--ar 16:9`
- Portraits work better with `--ar 2:3`

### For Other AI Image Generators:
- Remove Midjourney-specific parameters
- Keep the descriptive text
- Add "photorealistic, 8k, highly detailed, architectural photography" for emphasis
- Adjust based on specific model strengths

### Architectural Emphasis:
This story emphasizes architecture more than the first story. Key architectural elements:
- **The Factorepo Spire**: Glass, steel, spiraling, transparent, data as light
- **Archive District**: Historic stone, crumbling, romantic but dangerous
- **Meridian Bridge**: Modern, suspended, glass floor, LED lights
- **Enforcement Tower**: Brutalist, concrete, imposing, windowless
- **Corrective Facility**: White, clinical, modern, hospital-like

### Color Grading Suggestions:
- **Act One (Images 1-8)**: Warm golds and cool blues, architectural beauty
- **Act Two (Images 9-15)**: Transition to cooler tones, increasing tension
- **Act Three (Images 16-26)**: Stark whites, cold blues, clinical horror
- **Bonus (Images 27-30)**: Emphasize architectural beauty and control

### Character Consistency:
- **Kira**: Middle Eastern woman, late 20s, dark hair (bun or ponytail), professional attire, hazel eyes, strong features
- **Adrian**: Man early 30s, dark hair, striking blue eyes, casual but put-together, scar above left eyebrow
- **Enforcer Kade**: Stern, professional, modern tactical uniform
- **Dr. Moss**: Professional woman, white coat, gentle but clinical smile

### Consistency Tips:
- Maintain consistent character descriptions
- Keep architectural style grounded but futuristic
- Balance beauty with underlying control/surveillance
- Show the same locations at different times/moods
- Emphasize glass, transparency, and visibility as themes
