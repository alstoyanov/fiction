# The Distributed Truth - Novel #6

## Overview

**Series Position**: Book 6 of the Histologic Series (between "The Lost Hour" and "Battle of Truths")  
**Genre**: Political Philosophy Thriller / Peaceful Revolution  
**Status**: ✅ COMPLETE (First Draft - Needs Chapter Splitting)  
**Length**: ~90,000 words (24 chapters + prologue + epilogue)

## High Concept

Six months of fact warfare between Veridica and Chronos has fractured reality for border citizens. Marcus Chen is offered a corrupt but effective plan: infiltrate, seduce, and win through deception. Instead, he chooses radical transparency—exposing the Foundation's design and forcing transformation through distributed truth networks. The peaceful revolution succeeds through citizen choice, proving that exposure defeats weaponized certainty better than counter-weaponization.
