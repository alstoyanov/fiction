## Writing Guidelines for Full Chapters

### POV Discipline
- Each chapter has specific POV character
- Stay in that POV throughout
- Show their thoughts, feelings, perspective
- Other characters seen through their eyes

### Foundation Fragments
- Principle One: Detail fully in Chapter 1
- Principle Two: Detail fully in Chapter 2
- Integrate naturally into Marcus/Petra's research
- Show their reactions and implications

### Songs
- Integrate naturally (background, radio, characters singing)
- Don't force connections
- One per chapter maximum
- Enhance mood, don't explain theme

### Philosophical Debates
- Emerge from character conflicts
- No one "wins"
- Both positions have merit
- Characters question themselves

### Pacing
- Part One: Medium (establishing stakes)
- Part Two: Slow burn (seduction, intimacy)
- Part Three: Fast (execution, revelation)
- Part Four: Medium (consequences, setup)

### Tone
- Morally ambiguous
- No clear heroes or villains
- Victory feels hollow
- Guilt is pervasive
- Hope exists despite corruption
