## Synopsis

Kira Osman is a brilliant Senior Architect for Veridica's Factorepo Infrastructure—a true believer who has devoted her life to maintaining the system's perfection. When a charming stranger named Adrian saves her from a dangerous accident, she's drawn to him despite her usual caution. Adrian is an outlaw recruiter, sent to seduce and turn her, but unlike his previous targets, he falls genuinely in love with her.

When a critical security protocol fact that Kira personally entered into the factorepo mysteriously vanishes, she's accused of falsification or sabotage. The system she helped build turns against her. As she discovers Adrian is an outlaw and faces condemnation by The Judge, Kira begins to question everything: if facts can be stolen, can the infallible system be trusted?

Sent to the same corrective facility as Marcus Chen, Kira enters not repeating "I believe" but asking "What if the system made a mistake?"—the first step on a journey Marcus has not yet begun.

## Themes

## Story Structure

### Act One: The Meeting (25%)
Kira at work in the magnificent Factorepo Spire. Adrian saves her from falling stone in the Archive District. They begin dating. Romance develops as he shows her beauty she's been too busy to see.

Kira discovers the security protocol fact she entered is missing from the factorepo. Investigation begins. She's accused of falsification or sabotage. Adrian confesses he's an outlaw but swears he didn't steal the fact. Kira is condemned by The Judge.

### Act Three: The Correction (25%)
Kira confronts Adrian, learns the truth. She's sent to correction facility. Unlike Marcus, she's questioning: "What if the system made a mistake?" Adrian vows to find who stole the fact.

## The Stolen Fact - Technical Details

**What Was Stolen**: 
A security protocol update for factorepo data transmission encryption. Kira designed it, tested it, implemented it, and entered the fact of its completion. Now there's no record it ever existed.

**Why It Matters**: 
- Proves facts CAN be stolen from the "immutable" factorepo
- Kira's expertise makes her more suspicious (she COULD manipulate the system)
- Creates impossible situation: she knows the truth, system says otherwise
- If one fact can be stolen, any fact can be stolen
- Entire system's reliability is questioned

**Who Stole It**: 
Not revealed in this story. Mystery continues. Could be:
- Resistance (to expose system vulnerability)
- Internal corruption (someone in Veridica)
- Rival histonation (cyber warfare)
- Someone targeting Kira specifically

Adrian will investigate in future stories.
