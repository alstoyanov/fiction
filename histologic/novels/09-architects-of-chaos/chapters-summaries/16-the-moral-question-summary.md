Tanaka realizes: the architects genuinely believe they're healers. They see humanity as sick and themselves as doctors. That's why they can justify anything.

"I'm going to stop you," Tanaka says.

"I know," Maya replies. "And I'm sorry. Because when we're gone, the sickness returns. And millions will die. But you'll have your freedom. I hope it's worth it."
