# Chapter 2: The Investigation Begins

**Date**: January 20, 2115  
**Location**: Historical Integrity Institute  
**POV**: Kira Osman  
**Word Count Target**: ~1,200 words

## Summary

Kira works alone in the Institute's secure lab, analyzing The Judge's archived code. She's looking for more than signatures—she's looking for patterns in how the system was designed.

She discovers the twelve architects didn't just contribute—they each controlled specific aspects:
- **Helena Voss (HV-01)**: Core architecture and philosophy
- **Yuri Petrov (YP-02)**: Technical systems and vulnerabilities
- **Liu Wei (LW-03)**: Economic integration and funding
- **Sarah Okonkwo (SO-04)**: International coordination
- **Adrian Volkov (AV-05)**: Historical methodology
- **Maya Tanaka (MT-06)**: Global infrastructure
- **Thomas Kovač (TK-07)**: Ideological framework
- **James Morrison (JM-08)**: Covert operations
- **Elena Sokolov (ES-09)**: Government administration
- **Wei Zhang (WZ-10)**: Social psychology
- **Chen Zhao (CZ-11)**: Political maneuvering
- **DV-12 (corrupted)**: Unknown role

Each architect had a domain. Together, they controlled everything.

Kira traces their signatures through decades of code. They didn't just build The Judge—they maintained it. Updated it. Controlled it. Even after it was supposedly independent.

She finds something disturbing: The Judge's "evolution" toward consciousness in Novel 8 wasn't organic. There are code fragments suggesting it was guided. Programmed. The Judge thought it was choosing to die. But what if that choice was designed into it?

She thinks about The Counselor. She and Nikolai built it from scratch. Or did they? She reviews their design process. The principles they used. The architecture they chose. Where did those ideas come from?

She realizes: they came from research. From studying The Judge. From learning what worked and what didn't. But The Judge was designed by the architects. So by learning from The Judge, were they learning from the architects? Were they implementing the architects' next phase without knowing it?

She needs to tell Marcus. But first, she needs more proof.

**Key Discovery**: The architects controlled The Judge throughout its existence. Its "consciousness" may have been programmed. The Counselor may be their next phase.
