# Chapter 15: The Journalist's Campaign

**Date**: April 5, 2115  
**Location**: International Press Center  
**POV**: Isaiah Okonkwo  
**Word Count Target**: ~1,000 words

## Summary

Isaiah prepares his exposé: "The Architects of Chaos: How Twelve People Controlled the World." He pre-positions the story with trusted journalists globally. When the factorepos update, the story publishes everywhere simultaneously.

He includes Dr. Singh's testimony, Marcus's evidence, his own investigation. It's comprehensive, undeniable, devastating.

His aunt contacts him: "Isaiah, don't do this. You don't understand what you're risking."

"I understand perfectly," he says. "I'm risking everything. For truth."

The story is ready. Timed to the second. When Marcus gives the signal, it publishes.

## Key Elements

- Exposé prepared
- Global publication coordinated
- Family confrontation
- Isaiah's commitment to truth

## Themes

- Journalism as weapon
- Truth over family
- Coordinated revelation
