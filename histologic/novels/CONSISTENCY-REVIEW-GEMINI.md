## Inconsistencies Found & Fix List

### 1. The "Elena" Identity Crisis (The 4-Elena Problem)
**Severity**: High
**Issue**: The name "Elena" is overused, creating confusion about who is who.
*   **Elena Vasquez (Novel 1)**: Resistance Leader, Marcus's recruiter. **STATUS: KEEP**.
*   **Elena Volkov (Novel 3)**: Triplets' mother (Deceased). **STATUS: RENAME**.
*   **Elena Sokolov (Novel 9)**: General Sokolov's mother (Architect). **STATUS: RENAME**.
*   **Elena Chen (Novel 9 Epilogue)**: Marcus's daughter. **STATUS: KEEP** (Named after Vasquez).
*   **Novel 9 Confusion**: Summaries imply Resistance Leader "Elena" is a Volkov sibling. **STATUS: FIX**.

**Proposed Fixes**:
1.  **Rename Triplets' Mother**: Change Elena Volkov to **Sofya Volkov**.
2.  **Rename Sokolov's Mother**: Change Elena Sokolov to **Viktoria Sokolov**.
3.  **Clarify Resistance Leader**: Ensure she is consistently **Elena Vasquez**, unrelated to Volkovs.

### 2. The "Adrian Kovač" Characterization Conflict
**Severity**: High
**Issue**: Two conflicting characters named Adrian Kovač.
*   **Adrian Kovač (Novel 2)**: Outlaw/Recruiter, Kira's lover. Anti-system.
*   **Adrian Kovač (Novel 6/7)**: Senior Historian (Chronos), lonely target for seduction.
*   **Adrian Volkov (Novel 9)**: Architect (Deceased), Volkov uncle.

**Proposed Fixes**:
1.  **Rename Novel 6/7 Target**: Change the Chronos Historian from Adrian Kovač to **Julian Kovač** (or similar).
2.  **Clarify Adrian Volkov**: Ensure he is distinct from the outlaw Adrian Kovač.

### 3. Kira's Relationship Transition (Novel 4)
**Severity**: Medium
**Issue**: Kira falls in love with Adrian (Novel 2) but then falls for Marcus (Novel 4) without clear resolution of the first relationship.
**Proposed Fix**:
*   Add internal monologue in **Novel 4 (Chapter 2 or 16)** where Kira reflects on Adrian. She realizes his love was manipulative (part of recruitment) vs. Marcus's love which is shared trauma/truth. She feels betrayed by Adrian's deception about his identity.

### 4. General Sokolov's Rank
**Severity**: Low
**Issue**: Rank jump from Captain (Novel 3) to General (Novel 4) in ~5 years.
**Proposed Fix**:
*   Acceptable given wartime/border zone context, but ensure Novel 3 establishes her as "Fast-track" or "High Command Potential".
